## Enhancing Literature Reviews with AI: A Strategic Management Researcher's Guide

Literature reviews are the backbone of any rigorous management research project. Traditionally, they involve systematic searching, reading, synthesizing, and tracking hundreds of papers across multiple databases. AI can dramatically accelerate this process while maintaining academic rigor. Here's how.

---

## Phase 1: Smart Searching & Discovery

### Traditional Approach vs. AI-Augmented

| Traditional | AI-Augmented |
|------------|--------------|
| Manual keyword searches in Web of Science, Scopus, Google Scholar | AI suggests semantic search queries, related concepts, and paper clusters |
| Reading titles and abstracts one by one | AI summarizes abstracts, highlights relevance, ranks papers by fit |
| Snowballing citations manually | AI maps citation networks, identifies key papers and research streams |

### Practical Tools

1. **Connected Papers** - Visual citation networks to identify seminal works
2. **ResearchRabbit** - AI-powered paper discovery and recommendations
3. **Semantic Scholar** - AI-powered literature search with citation analysis
4. **Elicit.org** - Ask questions to literature, get evidence-based answers

---

## Phase 2: Efficient Reading & Synthesis

### AI-Assisted Reading

Instead of reading 50 papers linearly:

1. **Batch abstract review** - Use AI to extract key contributions, methods, and findings from 20-30 abstracts
2. **Targeted deep reading** - Select the 10-15 most relevant papers for full reading
3. **Thematic synthesis** - Ask AI to identify common themes, contradictions, and gaps

### Prompt Templates for Literature Synthesis

**Theme Identification:**
> "Here are 15 abstracts on CEO overconfidence and firm innovation. Identify the main theoretical perspectives, methods used, and key findings. Highlight contradictions and research gaps."

**Research Question Alignment:**
> "Based on these papers, how does CEO overconfidence influence R&D investment? What mechanisms are proposed? What contexts moderate this relationship?"

---

## Phase 3: Organizing & Writing the Review

### From Messy Notes to Structured Review

1. **Create a coding framework** (e.g., Theory, Method, Context, Findings, Gaps)
2. **Use AI to organize** extracted information into this framework
3. **Identify the conversation arc** - how has the literature evolved?
4. **Position your contribution** - where does your research fit?

### Writing with AI Support

**Drafting the Literature Review:**
> "Write a literature review section on CEO overconfidence and strategic decision-making. Organize by theoretical perspectives (upper echelons theory, behavioral theory of the firm), discuss key empirical findings, and identify gaps in understanding the boundary conditions. Include citations in APA style: [Author, Year]."

---

## 🔑 Key Principles for Academic Rigor

### DO's
- ✅ Use AI for discovery and synthesis, but verify everything
- ✅ Keep track of AI-assisted work in your research notes
- ✅ Verify AI-generated claims by reading the original papers
- ✅ Disclose AI use if required by your journal

### DON'Ts
- ❌ Let AI "hallucinate" citations - always verify sources
- ❌ Rely solely on AI summaries - critical reading is still essential
- ❌ Skip systematic database searches - AI should supplement, not replace
- ❌ Present AI-generated text as your own without review
